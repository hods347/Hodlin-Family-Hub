## How the inspector rated things

The report uses **Satisfactory / Marginal / Defective**, plus a "Notes" flag for
items to investigate with the sellers. Inspector language using **"NOW!"** or
rated **Defective** = act before/around closing. This plan maps those ratings to
priority and timing.

- **Defective (DEF)** → Urgent / High
- **Marginal (MAR)** → Medium
- **Notes / investigate** → Due-diligence (mostly free, do now)

**siding** (spot-repair vs. whole-house vinyl). Nailing down those two with
written estimates will collapse most of the uncertainty — and both are strong
candidates for **seller credit / price negotiation** given the Defective ratings.
